    About Jordan

    I'm an automation and AI engineer from Gostivar, North Macedonia. My path into development was unconventional — I joined Bechtel-Enka as a Document Control Clerk, got frustrated with how many things were done manually, and started fixing them. That pattern eventually turned into a full development project serving multiple departments.

    Outside of work, I build automation and AI systems. VideoForge is a multi-channel video-production platform that coordinates scripting, narration, visual production, editing, captioning, metadata, human review, publishing, and performance analytics from a single operator console. Player Contact Triage Automation is an audit-ready case-study prototype for classifying and routing regulated support messages with deterministic safety controls and structured audit outputs. Trend-Analysis is a content-intelligence dashboard that uses Claude with live web research to identify opportunities before they become saturated.

    I have a Bachelor's in Computer Science from University of Saint Apostol Pavel, specialising in Network and Database Security. I work primarily with Python, JavaScript, Node.js, and AI APIs — and I'm most comfortable turning complex operational workflows into controlled, auditable systems.
